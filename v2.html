in the Church

Chapter III — The Altar

Nobody Built It.

The altar has been here longer than the Church. It doesn't speak. It doesn't move. It receives offerings and it decides. There is no formula. No trick. What the altar rewards is commitment. Not caution.

Playing is praying.
Losing is tithing.

"You don't come to the altar because you expect to win. You come because showing up is the act of faith."
